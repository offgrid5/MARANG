Our Journey

  • Our company starts operations

    11 March 2020

    Founded with a vision to revolutionize Botswana's engineering sector, LTC Engineering began operations with a small team of experts dedicated to delivering world-class electrical solutions.

  • First major client

    19 June 2020

    Secured our first major contract with Botswana Power Corporation, establishing our reputation for reliability and technical excellence in high-voltage systems.

  • Team exceeds 50 people

    24 December 2021

    Expanded our team to over 50 skilled professionals, enabling us to take on larger and more complex projects across multiple industries.

  • International recognition

    15 October 2023

    Received the African Engineering Excellence Award for our innovative solar farm project that powers 20,000 homes in rural Botswana.
