    Our PPRA Grade E Certified Services

    High Voltage Reticulation

    Comprehensive high voltage solutions for industrial complexes, mining operations, and municipal infrastructure. Our Grade E certification ensures compliance with Botswana's strictest electrical standards.

    • 132kV transmission line installation
    • Substation design and construction
    • Preventative maintenance programs
    • BPC compliance certified

    Photovoltaic Systems

    Solar energy solutions designed for Botswana's climate. From residential installations to large-scale solar farms, we deliver PPRA-compliant systems with maximum efficiency and durability.

    • Commercial & industrial solar farms
    • Grid-tied and off-grid solutions
    • Battery storage systems
    • Government tender compliance

    Security Systems

    Integrated security solutions for government facilities, commercial properties, and residential complexes. Our PPRA Grade E certification ensures compliance with national security standards.

    • BOSSA-compliant CCTV installations
    • Access control systems
    • Perimeter security & alarm systems
    • Government building security

    Airfield Lighting

    CAAB-compliant airfield lighting systems for airports and airstrips. Our PPRA Grade E certification qualifies us for all government aviation projects across Botswana.

    • Runway edge & centerline lighting
    • Approach lighting systems (ALS)
    • PAPI & REIL installations
    • FAA/ICAO compliance
